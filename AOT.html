AOT SEASON 1:

In Attack on Titan season 1, humanity lives in fear of man-eating giants called Titans behind giant walls, a peace that is shattered when a Colossal Titan breaks through the outermost wall, Wall Maria. The protagonist,Eren Yeager, witnesses his mother being eaten and vows to kill every Titan. After joining the military with friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert, Eren discovers he has the mysterious ability to transform into a Titan himself. The season follows their training and first battles, which culminate in Eren using his power to fight a mysterious Female Titan, Annie Leonhart, revealing that other human-transforming Titans exist and that the walls themselves may be hiding a shocking secret.

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. After witnessing his mother’s death during the Titan invasion, he swore revenge on all Titans. He later discovers his own power to transform into a Titan, making him both a weapon and a mystery to humanity.

Armin Alert

Armin Arlert is a central character from the anime and manga series Attack on Titan, known for being the Colossal Titan and the commander of the Survey Corps. He is a childhood friend of Eren Yeager and Mikasa Ackerman, whose exceptional intelligence and strategic genius make him a vital asset despite his physical weakness. His journey is defined by his incredible growth from a timid boy to a capable leader who utilizes his strategic mind to save humanity.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is a central character in Attack on Titan who is a highly skilled soldier driven by a deep need to protect her adoptive brother, Eren Yeager. After her parents were murdered, she was taken in by the Yeager family, which profoundly changed her once-cheerful personality into a more stoic and withdrawn one, particularly when Eren is not around. She is an unparalleled genius who graduated at the top of her class in the Training Corps and joined the Survey Corps to stay by Eren's side

Titans:

Attack Titan

The Attack Titan is a 15-meter tall, muscular humanoid with a lean build, though its exact appearance varies depending on the inheritor. Key features include a jagged, often lipless mouth with exposed teeth, elongated ears, and deep-set eyes. Its hair and other facial features change to reflect the human host's age and characteristics over time, and it has a unique ability to access memories of both past and future inheritors.

Beast Titan

Zeke Yeager is the inheritor of the Beast Titan and a former member of Marley's Warrior Unit. His Titan form is an ape-like 17-meter giant with a powerful throwing ability, which he uses as artillery. Due to his royal blood, he can also use his spinal fluid to turn other Eldians into Titans and control them with a scream.

Armored Titan

The Armored Titan is a 15-meter class Titan from Attack on Titan, known for its ability to generate hardened, plated armor over its body for extreme durability against most attacks. While this armor is capable of withstanding cannon fire and high-speed blades, it significantly reduces the Titan's speed. This limitation can be overcome by selectively shedding the armor, particularly from the joints, to increase mobility. The user, Reiner Braun, can also use the armor to create sharp claws for scaling walls, or to charge and deliver powerful, high-impact blows.

Female Titan

The Female Titan from Attack on Titan is a 14-meter tall Titan with superhuman strength, speed, and endurance, known for its unique abilities: skin hardening and a screaming power that can attract other Titans. The only confirmed wielder is Annie Leonhart, a Marleyan warrior who uses her Titan for combat and to gather other Titans for a specific purpose. A unique capability of the Female Titan is its potential to mimic the abilities of other Titans by consuming parts of their bodies.

Colossal Titan

The Colossal Titan is the largest of the Nine Titans from the anime and manga Attack on Titan, known for its skinless, muscular body, immense size (around 60 meters tall), and the ability to release destructive steam and heat during transformation. It serves as a primary antagonist, capable of breaking through the Walls and causing mass destruction, though it is slow and lacks agility.

Cart Titan

The Cart Titan is one of the Nine Titans from Attack on Titan with remarkable endurance and speed, capable of remaining in its Titan form for months and transforming hundreds of times consecutively. It is unique for its quadrupedal, horse-like form and has a long, flat torso ideal for carrying cargo and weaponry. Its primary weaknesses are its relatively low defensive capabilities and slower regeneration compared to other Titans.

Jaw Titan

The Jaw Titan is one of the Nine Titans in Attack on Titan, known for its small size, exceptional speed, and powerful jaws and claws that can crush almost anything. Its abilities make it a potent assault unit. Previous inheritors include Marcel Galliard, Ymir, and Porco Galliard, while the most recent, Falco Grice, developed unique bird-like characteristics including wings, making his version the first and only flying Jaw Titan.

Warhammer Titan

The Warhammer Titan from Attack on Titan can create weapons and structures out of hardened Titan flesh, including its namesake warhammer, spikes, and flexible weapons like crossbows. Its user is encased in a crystal and can remotely control the Titan via a string of flesh, which makes the user invulnerable to capture as long as the crystal is intact and the string isn't severed. This unique control method makes the user's location irrelevant to the Titan's transformation location, but overusing the hardening ability can drain the user's stamina.

Founding Titan

The Founding Titan is one of the Nine Titans in Attack on Titan with the power to create and control Titans and modify the memories and biology of Eldians. It was the first Titan, created from Ymir Fritz, and has the ability to unleash the full power of the Walls and the Rumbling. While a royal family member is needed to unlock its full potential, anyone who touches a royal-blooded individual can use its powers.
